About Me

Hello! I'm Riley, a full-stack developer based in the USA. I have a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites and applications. My journey into web development started back in 2010 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes — turns out hacking together a custom reblog button taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of building software for an advertising agency, a start-up, a huge corporation, and a student-led design studio. My main focus these days is building products and leading projects for our clients at Upstatement.
